About Us

Black Rose Publishing House located in Toowoomba provides a platform to publish noir crime fiction by new authors with the goal of entering the professional writing field. We highly support emerging authors with the publishing and introducing their work globally. It was established in 2018 and been providing the supportive environment for new authors. Everyone needs an oppurtunity to demonstrate their potential. So we are here as a portal encouraging them to write the best without worrying about the publishing scenarios.
